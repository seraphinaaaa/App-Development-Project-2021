Shipping Guide

Find the necessary information about our shipping destinations, rates and other related information.

Shipping time is estimated and calculated from the data of shipping, not the date of order. International shipments can take longer than the expected date due to invalid address, customer clearance procedures or other unforeseen causes. In the event that your shipment does not arrive in time, please submit a support request within 3 months. Note that overdue requests may not be accepted.

Returns & Refunds

We gladly accept returns within 30 days of receipt for eligible items in good condition.

Important Notice:
1. Please do not send your return to the address on your package. This is not the return address and will affect processing your return.
2. If you have received a damaged/defective item, please submit a customer support form within 24 hours of receipt. Click here

Product and Stock
